Activity 10

Write a paragraph in about seventy words on the famous Bengali writer Bibhutibhusan Bandyopadhyay using the hints on page 15:

Bibhutibhusan Bandyopadhyay was a world-famous Bengali writer. He was born on 12th September 1894 to his father Mahananda Bandyopadhyay and mother Mrinalini Devi. He completed his early education at Bangaon High School. Later, he completed his intermediate and graduation from Ripon College, which is now known as Surendranath College in Kolkata. In his professional life, he worked as a Headmaster at Jangipara High School and later at Harinavi Anglo-Sanskrit Institution. He wrote many famous, masterpiece novels like Pather Panchali, Aparajito, Aranyak, Debjan, Icchamati, and Chander Pahar. This legendary writer passed away on 1st November, 1950.
