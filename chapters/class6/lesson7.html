Activity 3

Answer the following question:

"The king was, in his private profession, under the government." Is this king similar to the stories of other kings that you have read so far?

Answer: No, this king is not similar to the kings in traditional stories. Traditional kings are usually immensely wealthy, live in giant palaces, and rule their kingdoms, whereas this king is an ordinary working man who holds a private job under the government and struggles to buy food for his family.

Activity 5

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) Why did the king think that the old lady was a fairy?

Answer: The king thought the old lady was a fairy because she instantly guessed his thoughts, knew his name, and knew that he was the father of Princess Alicia and was heading to his office.

(b) What was the message given by Grandmarina?

Answer: Grandmarina’s message was that the king must invite Princess Alicia to eat the salmon at dinner, where she would leave a magic fish-bone on her plate. Alicia must dry, rub, and polish the bone until it shone like mother-of-pearl, and use its magic only once at the right time to get what she wished for.

(c) When did Grandmarina vanish?

Answer: Grandmarina vanished right after she delivered her message about the magic fish-bone to King Watkins.

(d) What did the king do in his office?

Answer: In his office, the king spent his time writing, writing, and writing, until it was finally time to go home.

Activity 7

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) Why was the king feeling miserable?

Answer: The king was feeling miserable because he was extremely poor, had no money at all to support his family, and saw no way of getting any.

(b) What was to Alicia the right moment for asking help from others?

Answer: To Alicia, the right moment for asking help from others was only after they had done their very best first, and found that even their best efforts were not enough.

(c) How did the king get his salary?

Answer: The king got his salary rattling down the chimney into the middle of the floor as soon as Alicia kissed the magic fish-bone.

(d) Why did Grandmarina wish to make an end of the fish bone?

Answer: Grandmarina wished to make an end of the fish-bone because its magic had already been used once, and she wanted to show that it could not be used again.
