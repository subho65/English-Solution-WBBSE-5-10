Activity 3

Answer the following question:

Boats and ships sail on the water, while clouds sail across the sky. State the difference in their manner of sailing.

Answer: Boats and ships are man-made structures that sail on rivers and seas, whereas clouds are natural formations that sail more beautifully and freely across the vast sky.

Activity 5

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) What is prettier than boats, ships, and clouds?

Answer: The rainbow (or the bow that bridges heaven) is prettier than boats, ships, and clouds.

(b) What are the clouds compared to?

Answer: The clouds sailing across the sky are compared to boats sailing on rivers and ships sailing on seas.

(c) How do you think the rainbow builds a road from the earth to the sky?

Answer: The rainbow is curved like a bridge, rising from the horizon on the earth and arching high up into the sky, which makes it look like it is building a road between the earth and the sky.

(d) Which, according to the poet, is the prettiest of all objects mentioned in the poem?

Answer: According to the poet, the rainbow (the bow that bridges heaven and builds a road from earth to sky) is the prettiest of all objects mentioned in the poem.

Activity 8 (a)

Write a paragraph in about sixty words describing the scene of a rainbow appearing after a heavy shower:

Yesterday afternoon, after a heavy shower, a beautiful rainbow appeared in the clear, cloudless sky. The sun had just come out, casting a warm glow on the wet leaves. It looked like a giant, colorful bridge of seven colors arching gracefully over the green treetops. Seeing its vibrant colors made me feel extremely happy and filled with wonder.

Activity 8 (b)

Suppose you are standing on a bridge looking down. Write a paragraph in about sixty words describing the things you see below:

Standing on a high bridge, I looked down at the wide river flowing calmly below. I could see small fishing boats sailing smoothly on the sparkling water. There were lush green fields on both banks, and some cattle were grazing peacefully nearby. It was a beautiful, relaxing sight that made me appreciate the peaceful charm of the countryside.
