Activity 3

Answer the following question:

Why did the moon appear small to Kepler from the space station?

Answer: The Moon appeared small to Kepler from the space station because the space station was moving far away from the Moon, making it look tiny in the vastness of space.

Activity 4

Write 'T' for true and 'F' for false statements in the given boxes. Give supporting statements for each of your answers:

(a) The Earth ferry was three times as big as the Moon ferries [ T ]

Supporting Statement: "It was three times as big as our Moon-ferries."

(b) There is hardly any water on the earth. — [ F ]

Supporting Statement: "The planet's all water!" I gasped.

(c) On Moon, oxygen is more easily available than water. — [ T ]

Supporting Statement: "On Moon, water was harder to get than oxygen."

(d) Kepler weighed six times more than his usual weight. — [ T ]

Supporting Statement: "I realized I weighed six times my normal weight."

Activity 5

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) Whose excited voices did Kepler hear?

Answer: Kepler heard the excited voices of the passengers from Earth who had arrived on the ferry.

(b) Why was water precious on Moon?

Answer: Water was precious on the Moon because there was hardly any water there, making it harder to get than oxygen, so people had to pay for every ounce of it.

(c) Why was Kepler feeling unwell?

Answer: Kepler was feeling unwell because of the heavy weight difference he experienced on entering Earth's gravity, as he weighed six times his normal weight.

(d) What led his father to help Kepler as they landed?

Answer: Since Kepler was feeling unwell and struggling with his own safety straps as they landed, his father leaned over to help him.

Activity 7

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) Who were waiting for the Governor of the Moon?

Answer: Reporters from the Press and TV were waiting for the Governor of the Moon.

(b) How, according to Kepler's father, could the difference between Earth and Moon people be resolved?

Answer: According to Kepler's father, the differences between Earth and Moon people could be resolved in a friendly and peaceful manner.

(c) How is Kepler's father attached to the Moon?

Answer: Kepler's father is attached to the Moon because his son was born there, his beloved wife was buried there, and his work is located there.

(d) Why did Kepler feel that earth would certainly be fun?

Answer: Kepler felt that Earth would certainly be fun because he could walk around outside freely without wearing a heavy space-suit.

Activity 10 (a)

Last night, I met a strange visitor from Mars in my backyard. He was tall, had green skin, and three large shining eyes. He spoke in a soft, musical tone and said he came in peace to explore Earth.
