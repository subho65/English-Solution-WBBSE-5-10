Activity 3

Answer the following question:

Why will the father and the child sing while ploughing, sowing and reaping?

Answer: The father and the child will sing while performing these agricultural tasks because they want to work happily, share their joy with nature, and seek blessings for a good harvest.

Activity 5

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) What are the three stages of farming?

Answer: The three stages of farming described in the poem are ploughing, sowing, and reaping.

(b) What are the different colours of the field at different stages of farming?

Answer: The field is green before ploughing, turns red during the sowing stage, and becomes brown when the reaping season arrives.

(c) What song do the wise men know?

Answer: The wise men know the seed-song sung by the father during the sowing stage.

(d) At what stage of farming is the scythe required?

Answer: The scythe is required at the final stage of farming, which is the reaping stage.
