Activity 3

Answer the following question:

"It had something to do with that ice cream." Why did the speaker think so?

Answer: The speaker thought so because Jerome Dadian, who was known to be a slow student, suddenly started scoring full marks in Maths after eating the special ice cream sold from Mr. Peppi's van.

Activity 4

Write "T" for true and "F" for false in the given boxes. Give supporting statements for each of your answers:

(a) Mr. Peppi sold ice creams of only one flavour. — [ F ]

Supporting Statement: "He sold ice cream, all different types. He had every flavour there is."

(b) The ice cream man was very unkind. — [ F ]

Supporting Statement: "He was always giving free ice cream to kids who had no money."

(c) The purple ice cream had no effect on the boy. — [ F ]

Supporting Statement: "He gave a purple ice cream to a boy with pimples, and his pimples were cured!"

(d) The speaker believed that there was no other boy smarter than him in school. — [ T ]

Supporting Statement: "I was the smartest kid in school and that's the way I wanted it to stay."

Activity 5

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) What did Mr. Peppi sell?

Answer: Mr. Peppi sold delicious, sweet ice creams of many different types and flavors from his ice cream van.

(b) How did the speaker know that Mr. Peppi was a soft-hearted person?

Answer: The speaker knew that Mr. Peppi was a soft-hearted person because he always gave free ice cream to poor children who could not afford to buy it.

(c) What was special about the green ice cream?

Answer: The green ice cream was special because it had a magical quality to make the person who ate it smarter.

(d) Why did the speaker want to do better than everyone else in the class?

Answer: The speaker wanted to do better than everyone else because he took great pride in being the smartest kid in the school and could not bear anyone else beating him.

Activity 7

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) What did the speaker find inside Mr. Peppi's ice cream van?

Answer: Inside Mr. Peppi's ice cream van, the speaker found many tubs of ice cream in different flavors, along with four special bins containing Happy, Nose, Pimple, and Smart Ice Creams.

(b) What secret about Dadian did the speaker come to know?

Answer: The speaker came to know that Dadian had been eating the special Smart Ice Cream, which was the secret behind his sudden high marks in Maths.

(c) Rewrite the last paragraph in normal English with correct spellings:

"It is the next day now. Something is happening to me. I don't feel quite as smart. I have been trying to do a real hard sum. It is one and one. What does one and one make? Is it three or is it four?"

(d) Why do you think there are peculiar spellings in the last paragraph? Give reasons for your answer.

Answer: There are peculiar spellings in the last paragraph to show that the speaker had eaten all of Mr. Peppi's Smart Ice Cream, which had an opposite, damaging effect on his brain, making him lose his smartness and forget how to spell or do basic calculations.

Activity 10 (a)

Write a story in about sixty words based on the given hints on page 115:

A proud hare always boasted about his fast speed and teased a slow tortoise. One day, they decided to run a race. The hare ran fast and saw the tortoise far behind. Feeling overconfident, he fell asleep midway. Meanwhile, the tortoise kept walking steadily. When the hare woke up, the tortoise was already near the finish line. The hare's last leap was too late, and the tortoise won the race.
