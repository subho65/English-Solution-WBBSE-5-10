  • (i) The accident between a speeding express and a goods train occurred near — (c) Gorakhpur

  • (iii) The train accident occurred around — (a) 10.35am

(i) The Express train was nearly 55km away from its destination when the accident took place. — [ F ]

Answer: A suitable title for the report could be: "Tragic Train Collision Near Gorakhpur Claims 22 Lives".
