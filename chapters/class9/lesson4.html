Exercise 2

Answer the following questions within fifteen words:

(1) How does the sky look as one is awakened by the birdsong?

Answer: The sky looks slightly tinged with yellow as one is awakened by birdsong.

(2) How do the parrots and parakeets salute the people?

Answer: They salute the people with a cacophony of loud and unpleasant sounds.

(3) What do all the animals do at the start of a new day?

Answer: All the animals bustle about their cages, excited and eager at the start of a new day.

(4) As the light fades, where does the robin fly off to?

Answer: As the light fades, the robin ceases to sing and flies off to roost in the mimosa tree.

Exercise 3

Answer the following questions within twenty-five words:

(1) What work do Jeremy and Mike do in the gorillas’ cages?

Answer: In the gorillas' cages, Jeremy and Mike sweep up the mess on the floor and scatter fresh white sawdust.

(2) Describe the state of the frogs and snakes in the reptile house.

Answer: In the reptile house, the snakes regard visitors calmly with their lidless eyes, while the frogs make gulping sounds.

(3) What would one see and hear while lying in bed at night?

Answer: While lying in bed, one would see the moon separating itself from the trees, and hear the lions coughing and the early morning birdsong.
