Activity 4

(1) Who was the Guru of Dronacharya?

Answer: Parasurama was the Guru of Dronacharya.

(2) Where was the bird placed?

Answer: The wooden bird was placed on a branch of one of the far-off trees in the woodland.

(3) What did Yudhisthira see?

Answer: Yudhisthira saw his guru, the tree, the people standing around him, and the wooden bird.

(4) Why did Dronacharya like Arjuna the most?

Answer: Dronacharya liked Arjuna the most because Arjuna always practiced the art of archery with intense concentration.

(5) What is most important in hitting a target?

Answer: Having absolute focus and concentration solely on the target, while shutting out all other distractions, is most important in hitting a target.
