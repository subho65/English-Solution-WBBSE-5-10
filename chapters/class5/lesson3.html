Let's Begin (Warm Up)

Q1. What do you see in the picture?

Answer: In the picture, we see a reproduction of a famous painting showing a man resting on a bed on a balcony with a woman sitting beside him, looking toward a monument in the distance.

Q2. What do you think the man is gazing at?

Answer: I think the man is gazing at the Taj Mahal.

Q3. Who is with the man?

Answer: A woman is sitting next to the man on the bed.

Activity 5

(1) Which country has a rich heritage of classical dance?

Answer: India has a rich heritage of classical dance.

(2) What is an essential part of Indian classical dance?

Answer: Dramatics is an essential part of the Indian classical dance tradition.

(3) Who loved to listen to the songs of Tansen?

Answer: Emperor Akbar loved to listen to the songs of Tansen.

(4) Which monument in India is among the seven wonders of the world?

Answer: The Taj Mahal is the monument in India that is among the seven wonders of the world.

(5) What is Buland Darwaza?

Answer: Buland Darwaza is a huge gateway built by Emperor Akbar at Fatehpur Sikri.

(6) Name the folk theatres of (a) Maharashtra, (b) Bengal, (c) North India.

Answer: The folk theatre of Maharashtra is called Tamasha, Bengal is called Jatra, and North India is called Nautanki.
