Class V English (Butterfly)

Lesson 6: The Clever Monkey — Activity Answers

Let's Begin (Warm Up)

Tell your partner the name of a story in which animals or birds appear as characters.

Answer: A famous story featuring animal characters is "The Tortoise and the Hare" from Aesop's Fables.

Look at the picture below. Can you guess what the story is all about?

Answer: Based on the picture of the monkey on the tree and the crocodile in the river, this story is about a clever monkey outsmarting a hungry crocodile.

Activity 1

(a) Where did the monkey live?

Answer: The monkey lived on the bank of a river.

(b) Why was there no danger for the monkey on the river island?

Answer: There was no danger for the monkey on the river island because there was hardly any other animal living on it.

(c) What was so attractive to the monkey?

Answer: The delicious, sweet, and juicy fruits growing on the island's trees were highly attractive to the monkey.

(d) Where did the crocodile live?

Answer: The crocodile lived on the far bank of the river with his wife.

(e) Who gave advice to the crocodile about catching the monkey?

Answer: The crocodile's wife gave him the advice on how to catch the monkey.

Activity 6

(a) Who swam to the rock?

Answer: The crocodile swam to the rock to lie still and wait for the monkey.

(b) Why did the monkey become suspicious?

Answer: The monkey became suspicious because the rock appeared much larger than usual, and it had not been that big when he arrived.

(c) What was the monkey's suggestion to the crocodile?

Answer: The monkey suggested that the crocodile open his mouth wide so that the monkey could jump directly into it.

(d) What did the monkey tell the crocodile from his safe shelter?

Answer: From his safe shelter on the tree, the monkey laughed and told the crocodile that while he was indeed bigger, he was also a much bigger fool for being tricked so easily.

Let's Talk (Dialogue Writing)

Complete the dialogue between the two monkeys:

1st Monkey: Hello! How are you?
2nd Monkey: Fine. How are you?
1st Monkey: I'm all right. Do you know about my adventure with the crocodile yesterday?
2nd Monkey: No, tell me please!
1st Monkey: A big crocodile was waiting on the rock to catch me.
2nd Monkey: But how did you return home?
1st Monkey: I wasn't afraid. I told him to open his mouth wide.
2nd Monkey: Then?
1st Monkey: I jumped on his head and then quickly leaped to the river bank.
2nd Monkey: Really? You're so clever!

Activity 13

Write a short story in eight sentences (The Clever Goat and the Foolish Fox):

  1. Once a fox was moving around a village and wasn't careful, so he suddenly fell into a deep well.
  2. He tried his best to get out but completely failed to do so.
  3. Soon, a thirsty goat came to the well and saw the fox inside.
  4. The cunning fox cleverly asked the goat to jump down and drink the delicious, sweet water from the well.
  5. The foolish goat believed the fox and jumped straight into the well.
  6. Taking advantage of this, the clever fox quickly climbed onto the goat's back.
  7. He leaped out of the well to safety and ran away.
  8. The poor, foolish goat was left behind and remained stuck in the well.
