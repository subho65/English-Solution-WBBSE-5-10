Let's Begin (Warm Up)

Where is the Charminar located?

Answer: The Charminar is located in Hyderabad, Telangana.

Which famous palace is located in Murshidabad?

Answer: The Hazarduari Palace is located in Murshidabad, West Bengal.

Where is the Golden Temple located?

Answer: The Golden Temple is located in Amritsar, Punjab.

Which famous monument is situated in Delhi?

Answer: The Qutab Minar is situated in Delhi.

Where is the Victoria Memorial situated?

Answer: The Victoria Memorial is situated in Kolkata, West Bengal.

Where is the Konark Sun Temple situated?

Answer: The Konark Sun Temple is situated in Konark, Odisha.

Do you know the name of any other famous monument?

Answer: Yes, the Taj Mahal in Agra is another world-famous monument.

Activity 4

(1) Where did the bus stop?

Answer: The bus stopped right in front of the Bandel Church.

(2) What were the names of the two friends of Mita?

Answer: The names of Mita's two friends were Amina and Sunita.

(3) How many Christians were taken to Agra fort?

Answer: A few thousand Christians were taken as prisoners to Agra fort.

(4) Who were made to stand before the ferocious elephants?

Answer: The Christian prisoners, including Father Joan da Cruz, were made to stand before the ferocious elephants.

(5) What did the elephant do with Father Joan da Cruz?

Answer: The elephant carried Father Joan da Cruz right up to the emperor and knelt before him as if asking for mercy.

(6) Where was the fort of Shah Jahan located?

Answer: The fort of Shah Jahan was located in Agra.
