Activity 5

(1) How was Usha's performance in New Delhi Asian Games?

Answer: In the 1982 New Delhi Asian Games, Usha performed exceptionally well, winning silver medals in both the 100 metres and the 200 metres sprint.

(2) Two Olympic Games are mentioned in the text. When did these take place? Where were these held?

Answer: The two Olympic Games mentioned in the text are the 1980 Moscow Olympics, held in Moscow, and the 1984 Los Angeles Olympics, held in Los Angeles.

Activity 6

(1) How old was Usha when she participated in the Seoul Asiad?

Answer: Usha was just 22 years old when she participated in the 1986 Seoul Asiad.

(2) Name the four events Usha won in the 1986 Seoul Asiad.

Answer: The four events Usha won in the 1986 Seoul Asiad were the 200 metres sprint, the 400 metres sprint, the 400 metres hurdles, and the 4x400 metres relay race.

(3) How popular is Usha in her state?

Answer: Usha is incredibly popular in her state; she became a legendary icon for women athletes, and newborn babies in Kerala were regularly named after her.
