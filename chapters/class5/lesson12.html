Let's Begin (Warm Up)

What do you see in the picture?

Answer: In the picture, we see the planet Earth as viewed from outer space.

Which colours do you see in the picture?

Answer: I see blue, white, brown, and green colors in the picture.

What does the blue colour signify?

Answer: The blue color signifies the vast oceans and water bodies that cover most of the Earth's surface.

Activity 4

(a) Where was Kalpana Chawla born?

Answer: Kalpana Chawla was born in Karnal, in the state of Haryana.

(b) Where did Kalpana go in 1982?

Answer: In 1982, Kalpana went to the USA to study aeronautical engineering at the University of Texas at Arlington.

(c) What was Kalpana called by her family?

Answer: Kalpana was affectionately called "Montu" by her family.

(d) What was the name of the space shuttle that was lost?

Answer: The name of the space shuttle that was lost was Columbia.

(e) When did Kalpana die?

Answer: Kalpana died on 1st February, 2003 when her space shuttle disintegrated during re-entry.

(f) What has been named after Kalpana?

Answer: A hill on Mars and a star in deep space have been named in honor of Kalpana Chawla.

Activity 9

Write six connected sentences about your experience of seeing the night sky:

  1. Last night, I went up to the rooftop of our house around 10:00 p.m. to look at the sky.
  2. The sky was very clear, dark, and filled with thousands of twinkling, bright stars.
  3. I was thrilled to see the silver moon shining beautifully like a giant pearl among the stars.
  4. Looking closely, I also saw a tiny, fast-moving satellite and a beautiful shooting star crossing the sky.
