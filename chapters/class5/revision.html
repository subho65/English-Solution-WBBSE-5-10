  • (1) Grandfather told the boy a story. — [ F ]
  • (2) The boy fell asleep while listening to the story. — [ T ]
  • (3) The angel's face looked like the face of the child's mother. — [ F ]
  • (4) The boy woke up at the call of his grandfather. — [ T ]

Activity 1 [B]

(a) How did grandmother describe the angel?

Answer: Grandmother described the angel as a beautiful figure who possessed bright, shining wings.

(b) Why do the angels take children on a tour?

Answer: The angels take children on a tour of the sky if the children have been exceptionally good.

(c) What were the beautiful things the boy saw in his dream?

Answer: In his dream, the boy saw sparkling stars, a bright moon, and the calm, beautiful face of the angel.

(d) What did the boy understand when he woke up?

Answer: When the boy woke up at his grandfather's call, he understood that his wonderful tour of the sky was actually just a dream.

Let's Talk (Quiz Answers)

  • What is the capital city of West Bengal? → Kolkata is the capital city of West Bengal.
  • What is the capital of India? → New Delhi is the capital of India.
  • What is the capital city of Bihar? → Patna is the capital city of Bihar.
  • Which state has Ranchi as its capital? → Jharkhand has Ranchi as its capital.
  • Can you say which state has Mumbai as its capital city? → Maharashtra has Mumbai as its capital city.
  • What is the capital city of Tamil Nadu? → Chennai is the capital city of Tamil Nadu.
  • Where is Hyderabad situated? → Hyderabad is situated in the state of Telangana.
  • Which city is called the 'Pink City'? → Jaipur is called the 'Pink City'.

Activity 12

Write five sentences for each season:

[I] Summer Season:

  1. Summer brings extreme heat and scorching sunshine to our country.
  2. During this season, we sweat heavily and often feel a deep thirst.
  3. We use fans to keep ourselves cool indoors.
  4. People enjoy eating sweet and juicy mangoes during the hot days.
  5. Heavy thunderstorms occasionally occur in the late afternoon to bring relief.

[II] Winter Season:

  1. Winter is a cold season characterized by chilly winds and morning mist.
  2. We wear warm woollens and use thick quilts to stay comfortable.
  3. Sitting in the pleasant sunshine is a favorite pastime for many.
  4. Colorful flowers like dahlias and marigolds bloom in gardens.
  5. It is also a wonderful time for family picnics and eating fresh oranges.
