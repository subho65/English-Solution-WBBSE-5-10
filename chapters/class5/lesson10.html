Lesson 10: A Great Social Reformer — Activity Answers

Let's Begin (Warm Up)

Who are these people?

Answer: These people are Raja Rammohan Roy, Swami Vivekananda, Henry Louis Vivian Derozio, and Ishwar Chandra Vidyasagar, who are famous social reformers of India.

Why are they famous?

Answer: They are famous because they dedicated their lives to educating people, reforming society, and abolishing harmful social evils.

Let's Match Column A with Column B

  • Vidyasagar → started widow remarriage
  • Vivekananda → spread the message of brotherhood
  • Raja Rammohan Roy → stopped the evil custom of 'Sati'
  • Derozio → started Young Bengal Movement
