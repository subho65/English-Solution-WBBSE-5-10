Lesson 4: Memory in Marble — Activity Answers

Let's Begin (Warm Up)

What do you see in the picture?

Answer: In the picture, we see the Taj Mahal, a magnificent white marble monument situated in Agra.

What is the colour of the building?

Answer: The colour of the building is white.

Activity 1

  • (a) Tarun's grandfather knew a lot about the Taj Mahal because he was a retired teacher.
  • (b) Akbar the Great was the grandfather of Prince Khurram.
  • (c) Khurram first saw Arjumand Banu Begum at the Meena Bazaar.

Activity 7

Write five sentences about a place of interest you have visited:

  • 1. Last summer vacation, I visited the Victoria Memorial in Kolkata with my family.
  • 2. It is a stunning historical monument constructed entirely out of white marble.
  • 3. Inside the grand building, we saw beautiful old paintings, statues, and weapons.
  • 4. The monument is surrounded by spacious, well-maintained green gardens and water pools.
  • 5. It was a wonderful and highly educational experience that I will always remember.

Activity 8

Write five sentences about your journey from your home to school:

  • 1. The total distance from my home to my school is about two kilometers.
  • 2. My daily mode of travelling is by school bus along with my classmates.
  • 3. On our way, we regularly pass by a beautiful playground, a post office, and a busy market.
  • 4. The most exciting part of my journey is talking and laughing with my friends on the bus.
  • 5. Looking out of the window at the busy streets and tall trees makes the ride very pleasant.
