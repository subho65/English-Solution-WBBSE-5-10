Lesson 8: The Snail — Activity Answers

Exercise 1

Choose the correct alternative to complete the following sentences:

  • (a) With the slightest touch, the snail shrinks into its house with — (i) displeasure
  • (b) In its house, the snail lives with — (iv) no one

Exercise 2

State whether the following statements are True or False. Provide sentences/phrases/words in support of your answer:

(a) The snail fears to fall from the wall. — [ False ]

Supporting Statement: "To grass, or leaf, or fruit, or wall / The snail sticks close, nor fears to fall,"

(b) The snail comes out of his house during a storm. — [ False ]

Supporting Statement: "Within that house secure he hides / When danger imminent betides / Of storm, or other harm..."

Exercise 3

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) What does the snail usually stick itself to?

Answer: The snail usually sticks itself close to grass, leaf, fruit, or wall.

(b) What makes the snail well-satisfied?

Answer: The snail is well-satisfied to be his own whole treasure, living completely alone with no personal belongings.

Exercise 4

Change the following sentences into questions, as directed:

  • (a) Siraj always rises early. (Interrogative sentence using ‘does’)
    Answer: Does Siraj not always rise early? (or Does Siraj always rise early?)
  • (b) Joyce is the best singer in the class. (Information question using ‘who’)
    Answer: Who is the best singer in the class?
  • (c) He saw the rainbow. (Interrogative sentence using ‘did’)
    Answer: Did he not see the rainbow? (or Did he see the rainbow?)
  • (d) I go to school by bus. (Information question using ‘how’)
    Answer: How do you go to school?
