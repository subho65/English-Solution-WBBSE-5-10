Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) Where did Father put the letter?

Answer: Father put the letter in an envelope and sealed it securely.

(b) What did Swami fail to decide about Samuel?

Answer: Swami failed to decide if Samuel really deserved the serious allegations made against him in his father's letter.

(c) How did Samuel look?

Answer: Samuel had a dark face, a thin moustache, unshaven cheeks, and wore a distinctive yellow coat.
