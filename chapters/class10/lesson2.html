Exercise 3

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) Who had a quarrel with the squirrel?

Answer: The mountain had a quarrel with the squirrel.

(b) What is not a disgrace to the squirrel?

Answer: To occupy its small physical place on the earth is not a disgrace to the squirrel.

(c) What is it that the squirrel doesn’t deny?

Answer: The squirrel does not deny that the mountain makes a very pretty track for squirrels.

Exercise 5

  • (a) Lock the door.
    Answer: Let the door be locked.
  • (b) Ashim knows the solution to this problem.
    Answer: The solution to this problem is known to Ashim.
  • (c) I had written a letter.

Exercise 7

Write a paragraph (within 100 words) on the benefits of early morning exercises using the given points:

Taking part in early morning exercises is highly beneficial for both our physical and mental health. Rising early and exercising in the fresh morning air revitalizes our mind and body, providing us with a positive start to the day. It improves our blood circulation, strengthens our muscles, and keeps us active and energetic throughout the busy hours. Additionally, breathing clean, unpolluted air in the morning keeps respiratory ailments away. In conclusion, maintaining a regular routine of early morning exercise is a key step toward leading a healthy, fit, and disease-free life.
