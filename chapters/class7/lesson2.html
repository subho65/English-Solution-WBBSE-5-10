Lesson 2: The Riddle — Activity Answers

Let's Share

Can you name any other famous emperor of India?

Answer: Yes, Emperor Ashoka is another highly famous emperor of India.

Why would a person be called a ‘gem’?

Answer: A person is called a 'gem' when they possess extraordinary talent, wisdom, or rare qualities that make them extremely valuable to society.

Can you name any other ‘gem’ of Akbar’s court other than Birbal?

Answer: Tansen, the legendary musician, was another famous 'gem' of Akbar’s court.

Activity 7

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) What were the things that the great Emperor Akbar loved?

Answer: The great Emperor Akbar loved arts, sciences, literature, and music.

(b) Why did Akbar not have peace of mind?

Answer: Akbar did not have peace of mind because he was troubled by a simple riddle—"what is the fastest thing on this planet?"—to which he could not find an answer.

(c) What was the problem presented by the man from the audience?

Answer: The man presented a problem that his neighbor was charging him money to draw water from a well he had purchased, claiming he had only sold the well itself, not the water inside.

(d) How did Birbal solve the problem?

Answer: Birbal told the neighbor that if he had kept his water in the sold well, he must immediately pay rent to the buyer for keeping his water there, or remove his water at once. This logical argument forced the neighbor to withdraw his claim.

Activity 11

Write a dialogue in about 75 words where you solve a riddle for your friend:

Sunil: Hello Rohit! What’s going on?
You: Nothing serious. Just having a crack at this crossword.
Sunil: Good to see that you like puzzles. Well, here’s a riddle for you.
You: What’s the riddle?
Sunil: Well, what has keys but can't open any locks, and has space but no room?
You: That's quite an interesting one! Let me think for a moment.
Sunil: Give up?
You: Oh, I got it! It's a computer keyboard. It has keys and a space bar, but no locks or rooms!
Sunil: Excellent! You solved it so quickly.

Activity 12

Write a story in about 80 words (The Clever Fox and the Foolish Crow):

A crow was flying for a long time. He became very hungry. Fortunately, he found a delicious piece of meat and flew to a tree branch to eat it peacefully. A clever fox passing by noticed the crow and wanted the meat. He praised the crow's sweet voice and asked him to sing. The foolish crow opened his beak to sing, and the meat fell to the ground. The fox quickly grabbed the meat and ran away happily, leaving the sad crow behind.
