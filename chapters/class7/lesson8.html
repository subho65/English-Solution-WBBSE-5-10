Activity 8

Answer the following questions in complete sentences:

(a) Why did Ceres feel very sad in the morning?

Answer: Ceres felt very sad because her fair daughter Proserpine had disappeared, and despite searching all night, she could not find her.

(b) Why was there a severe famine all over the world?

Answer: There was a severe famine because Ceres, in her deep grief, neglected all her work on earth, causing the crops to fail and the ground to become dry and barren.

(c) Briefly narrate what the nymph of the fountain told Ceres.

Answer: The nymph of the fountain told Ceres that she lived in the deep earth and had seen Proserpine sitting on a throne in the kingdom of Pluto, and advised Ceres to pray to Jupiter to get her daughter back.

(d) Why was Proserpine bound to live for six months in the underground kingdom?

Answer: Proserpine was bound to live for six months in the underground kingdom because she had eaten six pomegranate seeds there, with each seed representing one month in the realm of the dead.
